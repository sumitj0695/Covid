Get vaccinated to defeat COVID-19!
"Get yourself and your loved ones vaccinated to protect yourselves against the coronavirus. With vaccines, we can win!"
How do vaccines protect you?
Vaccines boost your immunity and help your body fight the virus better
They reduce the risk of spreading the virus to your contacts
They help in avoiding serious complications even if you get the virus
How to get the vaccine?
  • 1
    Register/Sign In on cowin.gov.in using a Photo ID proof
  • 2
    You can add up to 4 members, using the same mobile number
  • 3
    Search for vaccination centres using PIN or District and book slot
  • 4
    Carry the Photo ID proof you used while registering to the vaccination centre

Post vaccination
Any discomfort is normal. Don't panic and consult a doctor
Remember to get the second dose of the vaccine
Continue to wear masks, maintain social distancing and sanitize your hands regularly
Do not go out, unless necessary
Encourage others to get vaccinated
